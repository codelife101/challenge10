frisbee can

First go see Jake to collect a frisbee Head down to the beach and you will find the set up. Enter the beach from the main entrance and look for the drift wood standing up.Fyi your team will need to get wet to get to it.


  • First off you will need a team member to find three cans that are buried within the rectangle marked in the sand. If you wish to you can change team members but only one team member is allowed to be searching at a time.

  • Once found you then need to place the can on top of a post thingy that Jake has hacked together. There are four posts you will use one. If another team shows up they will stand at a opposite end so that turns can be take with the frisbee. Your team will need to call Jake, once you are at the drift wood, so he can come down and gift you a post.

  • You then need to stand behind the line and try knock the can off by throwing the frisbee. Once the can has knocked off the drink needs to be sunk and then repeat by placing the next can on the post.

  • Once all three cans have been knocked off and sunk your team is free to move on.
